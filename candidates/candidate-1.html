No. 02 — Our Story Est. in a home kitchen

Born small, in a kitchen in Gilbert.

Meghan’s Little Café started the way the best things do — small. A love of slow mornings, good coffee, and a quiet corner of Gilbert, Arizona to share it from.

Every latte is pulled by hand, one at a time, with the milk and flavors you choose. No rush, no fuss — just simple joys you can taste. The menu stays short on purpose while we’re little, and baked goods are on their way.
