( nº 02 — the print )

Eight pours,
pressed like leaves

Meghan sketches her favorite pours the way you’d press wildflowers — dated, numbered, kept. Each leaf on this print is a real latte that left the porch counter one spring morning.

It hangs in the kitchen as a quiet promise: every cup gets drawn by hand, never poured twice the same.

( nº 03 — our little story ) slow living, simple joys

It started the way the best things do — small. A home kitchen in Gilbert, a love of slow mornings, and a porch to share them from.

Every latte is pulled by hand, one at a time, with the milk and flavors you choose. No line, no rush, no fuss — just the hiss of the machine and a cup made for exactly one person: you.

We’re keeping the menu short on purpose while we’re little. Baked goods are on their way, and until then the lattes get all of our attention.

slow living — fresh air — wildflowers — homemade — cozy spaces — simple joys
