Our little story

Born in a home kitchen, in Gilbert.

Meghan’s Little Café started the way the best things do — small. A love of slow mornings, good coffee, and a quiet corner of Gilbert, Arizona to share it from.

Every latte is pulled by hand, one at a time, with the milk and flavors you choose. We’re keeping the menu short on purpose while we’re little — baked goods are on their way — and when your drink is ready, it’s waiting for you at the front-porch counter.
