our little story

Born small, in a Gilbert home kitchen.

Meghan’s Little Café started the way the best things do — small. A love of slow mornings, good coffee, and a quiet corner of Gilbert, Arizona to share it from.

Every latte is pulled by hand, one at a time, with the milk and flavors you choose. No line of machines, no rush — just slow living you can taste, handed to you at the front-porch counter.
