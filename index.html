About Us

Meg’s Café started the way the best things do — small. A love of slow mornings, good coffee, and a quiet corner of Gilbert, Arizona to share it from.

Every latte is pulled by hand, one at a time, with the milk and flavors you choose. No rush, no fuss — just simple joys you can taste.
