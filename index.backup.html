our little story

Born in a home kitchen, in Gilbert.

Meghan’s Little Café started the way the best things do — small. A love of slow mornings, good coffee, and a quiet corner of Gilbert to share it from.

Every latte is pulled by hand, one at a time, with milk and flavors you choose. No rush, no fuss — just simple joys you can taste. We’re keeping the menu short on purpose while we’re little (baked goods are on their way!).
